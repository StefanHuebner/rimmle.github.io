About

If you are searching for high quality mobile app development at reasonable pricing, then we are the right address! Worldwide there are plenty of IT companies, especially in Asian regions. This makes it hard to filter the professional and trustable ones.

We are an IT-specialized agency based in Germany and India. A special relationship between both countries allows us to provide efficient and respectable mediation work. Our intention is to connect you to our partner in India which is renowned to provide IT solutions on international level. All our partner has longterm business experience - especially in USA, UK and Australia. Our focus is to get you in touch with a trustable IT company which will provide reliability and quality at reasonable prices.

We are aware that due to various aspects like language barriers, mentality differences or different legal systems, doing business abroad can appear risky and unsafe. In order to eliminate those doubts we would like you to have a closer look at our references and cooperation partners.

Are you interested in deeper insights and further information ? Just send us an email or make a call!

The Process

Only five steps to your dream

  • 1. Rough planning

    Let’s get started!

    We collect all the requirements of your project in order to calculate estimated time of delivery and price. For this you can use the questionnaire on our home page.

  • 2. Detail planning & drafting

    Shape to your vision

    Often just the visualisation of a project can be challenging. We will take care of this part! As per the requirement, our designers will present a few mockups best suited for your project. Mockups will also help you to select colors scheme, font style and layout design. After your final approval our developers will take up the work.

  • 3. Development

    Sit back and relax!

    While our team is working on your project, you can track the progress online on a development server and share your feedback.

  • 4. Testing

    Almost done

    Get ready while we are testing your product on all popular browsers and mobile devices to guarantee a smooth launch.

  • 5. Support

    We are live!

    While you are focussing on your future endeavors, we are here to backup your project with any technical assistance, expansion, updates and security assurance.
